About me

I'm an AI Engineer and Researcher at the Greek National Centre of Scientific Research 'Demokritos', specializing in neurosymbolic AI and machine learning applications in biomedical research. I hold a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens.

My work focuses on developing hybrid ML approaches that combine neural networks with symbolic reasoning for complex event recognition, cancer progression modeling, and forecasting applications.
